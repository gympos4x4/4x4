Funguje to pri takomto zapojeni na leonarde zo SOSu

MRF UNO:
	2 (RESET) 	-> 13
	4 (INT)		-> 2 (interrupt 1)
	5 (SDI)		-> MOSI
	6 (SCK)		-> SCLK
	7 (SDO)		-> MISO
	8 (CS)		-> 11

MRF LEONARDO:
	2 (RESET) 	-> 8
	4 (INT)		-> 3 (interrupt 0)
	5 (SDI)		-> MOSI
	6 (SCK)		-> SCLK
	7 (SDO)		-> MISO
	8 (CS)		-> 10
